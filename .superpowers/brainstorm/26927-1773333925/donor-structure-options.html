How Should Donors Be Structured?

Three approaches for where and how donor info lives in the dashboard. Click your preference.

A

Dedicated Donors Tab (5th tab)

Donors get their own tab in the Internal Dashboard alongside Overview, Loans, NIL Deals, Athletes. Full investor-style view: each donor card shows name, amount invested, % of total fund, projected return (5% + principal), and which loan they're tied to.

Pros

  • Clean separation
  • Easy to scan all donors at once

Cons

  • One more tab to navigate
B

Donors Inline Within Loans Tab

No separate tab. Each loan row in the Loans tab expands to show the donor who funded it — name, amount, % share, and projected return. Donors are always seen in context of the loan they funded.

  • Fewer tabs

  • Harder to see all donors at a glance
C

Both — Donors Tab + Inline on Loans

Donors get their own tab for a full portfolio overview, AND each loan in the Loans tab shows its donor inline. Best of both worlds — you can browse donors independently or see them in context.

Pros

  • Most complete view
  • Flexible navigation

Cons

  • More to build
  • Some info duplicated
